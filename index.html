City of Mandaluyong Science High School

City of Mandaluyong Science High School (CMSHS) is a public science high school located in Mandaluyong City, Philippines. Established in 1996, CMSHS is known for its rigorous academic programs and emphasis on science and technology education. The school aims to nurture students with exceptional talents in science and mathematics, preparing them for careers in various scientific fields.

MEDIA AND ACIEVEMENTS


Très bien, Batmans!

In July 2023, ten students from the City of Mandaluyong Science High School (CMSHS) took the Diploma of Studies in French Language (DELF) A1 examination at the Alliance Française de Manille, guided by their French teachers Mrs. Jonafe Jynn Tanalgo and Mr. Chris Patron, along with principal Mrs. Gigi Bullanday. The exam tested their French proficiency through four sections: oral comprehension, reading comprehension, written production, and oral production.



2025 Division Science & Technology Fair

The City of Mandaluyong Science High School (CMSHS) dominated the 2025 Division Science and Technology Fair at Senate President Neptali A. Gonzales Integrated School on October 8, sweeping all 10 categories
in both individual and team Science Investigatory Project (SIP) divisions. The winning students, coached by Crizel Peligrino and John Francis Cultura, will advance to the Regional Science and Technology Fair in November.
CMSHS also excelled in other events, with Nathan Teves and Rudolf Taguba winning gold in the senior high Science Quiz Bee, the junior high team placing second, and Danica Constante earning third place in the Art of the Atom Poster Making Contest.

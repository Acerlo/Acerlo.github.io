SCHOOL EVENTS


Buwan ng WIKA

Before, sections of classes were named after Filipino (Second and Fourth Year) and foreign scientists (First and Third year). In 2009, sections of classes were named after elements in the Periodic table in School year 2013-2014, sections were named after constellations, scientists, units of measurement, and molecular and atomic parts.




Teachers Day

CMSHS values its teachers deeply, and by that, every year during the celebrations of Teacher's Day, students are handpicked by teachers to teach in their stead so that other students may still learn while the teachers take a well-deserved break. This year, the City of Mandaluyong Science High School (CMSHS) celebrated National Teachers' Month 2025 with a "Stellar"-themed program on October 3 at the school grounds. Organized by the Supreme Secondary Learner Government (SSLG), the three-hour celebration featured a teachers' parade with medal awards, student teacher recognition, video tributes, gift-giving ceremonies, and musical performances by students, concluding with individual classroom celebrations.




Science Camp

CMSHS celebrates various events throughout the academic year, including Science Week, Foundation Day, and Cultural Festivals. These events provide opportunities for students to showcase their talents, engage in scientific exploration, and foster school spirit. The school also hosts guest lectures, workshops, and extracurricular activities that enrich the student experience.




Earthquake Drill

City of Mandaluyong Science Highschool frequently holds drills to prepare students for natural disasters and calamities that may happen at any time. The school uses roads and paths near it as evacuation directions for students, teachers, and faculty members.




YAPAK

To promote various movements like environmental or social acts that benefit students and spread awareness, CMSHS hosts events like schoolwide walks to promote mental health for students, teachers, faculty members.
